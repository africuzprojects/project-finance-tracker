Currency & FX

Amounts are stored in book currency using “units of each currency per 1 USD”.

Live refresh uses ExchangeRate-API (open.er-api.com), then ECB rates via Frankfurter, then a third fallback. Defaults are placeholders until you refresh or edit. Saved data keeps previous numbers until you refresh.
